About Callie

Welcome to my travel blog! Join me and my furry companion, a pet rabbit named Callie Furmint, as we explore new destinations together. From hopping through the bustling streets of Paris to relaxing in the sun-soaked beaches of Bali, we're on a mission to discover the world's most pet-friendly destinations. Follow us as we share our adventures, tips on traveling with a rabbit, and adorable photos of our furry friend. Let's go on an adventure together!
